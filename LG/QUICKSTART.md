## Welcome to Lead Generation!

Welcome to the Lead Generation department at Remote Helpers! This guide will help you get started quickly and understand your role.

## Your Role

As a member of the Lead Generation team, you'll be responsible for:

1. Analyze lead visibility patterns and CRM data
2. Set up automated lead generation workflows
3. Execute cold outreach campaigns
4. Generate and qualify leads via web scraping
5. Manage CRM systems and lead databases
