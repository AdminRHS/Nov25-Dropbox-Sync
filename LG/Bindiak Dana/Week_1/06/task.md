## Task 3: Job Sites Task - Finding and Adding Relevant Companies to CRM

### Steps:
1. Search and identify relevant companies on job sites
2. Evaluate each company for relevance to project objectives
3. For relevant companies, use RE extension to create company cards in CRM system
4. Utilize SalesQL extension to gather contact information from companies' LinkedIn pages
5. Identify potential leads from each company
6. Verify unclear position titles using ChatGPT
7. Translate position titles using Deepl translation tool where necessary

9. Send connection requests to identified leads across LinkedIn working accounts
10. Periodically switch between LinkedIn accounts during task execution
11. Document total number of company cards created

**Workflow:**
