## Task 2: Job Sites Task - Lead Generation and CRM Management

### Steps:
1. Review and select relevant companies from Job Sites
2. Add companies to CRM system using Re-E extension (new version)
3. Collect contact information from leads' LinkedIn profiles using SalesQL extension
4. Verify job titles and company roles using ChatGPT and DeepL translation tools
5. Add collected contact details to CRM system with accurate position information
6. Send connection requests to all leads added to CRM
7. End-of-day review: check new connections and send first message templates
8. Update statuses in CRM system for all new connections

### Resources and Links:
- Job Sites (source for companies)
- Re-E Extension (new version - for adding companies to CRM)
- SalesQL Extension (for collecting LinkedIn contact information)
- ChatGPT (for verifying job titles)
- DeepL Translation Tool (for translating and verifying company roles)
- CRM System
- First Message Template

### Instructions:

Begin by reviewing Job Sites to identify relevant companies. Use the Re-E extension (new version, previously tested and efficient) to add selected companies to the CRM system.

For each lead, use SalesQL extension to collect their contact information directly from their LinkedIn profile. To ensure accuracy of position identification, cross-reference job titles and company roles using ChatGPT and DeepL translation tools whenever verification is needed.

Once all lead information is accurately added to the CRM, send connection requests. At the end of the day, review all new connections that were made, send them the first message template, and update their statuses in the CRM system.

Goal: Connect with as many relevant leads as possible while maintaining data accuracy and consistent communication.
