**Gmail Account Setup**  
Open the Google search results page.  
Click on the **Google account icon** in the top right corner of the page.

**![][image1]**  
In the dropdown menu, select More settings.  
**![][image2]**  
Go to Privacy & Safety.  
Under the Content section, turn off Search personalisation.  
**![][image3]**

Next, navigate to Other settings.  
Select Language and Region.  
**![][image4]**In the Display language, choose English.  
Under the Result region, select the country where you want to search for companies.  
**![][image5]**  
Click **Confirm** to save your settings
