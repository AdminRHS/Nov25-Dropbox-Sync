**New Lead Generation Process Concept**

The new approach shifts focus from mass contact addition to deeply working on each lead who has shown interest. Template responses are abolished.

**New Lead Generation Workflow (Upon Receiving a Response):**

1. **Deep Research**:  
   * A mandatory company analysis. This is not just using AI functions, but developing the skill of understanding the business.  
   * **What to Analyze**: Website (About Us, Case Studies, Footer), Social Media (posts, videos, activity), reviews, mentions online.  
   * **Goal**: Understand the product, market, decision-makers, and identify "gaps" (weak design, lack of media content).  
   * **Tools**: Manual analysis, structured queries to Claude, Gemini, ChatGPT, Perplexity.  
2. **Lead Magnet Generation**:  
   * Based on identified needs, generate relevant value in a single shot (e.g., presentation in Gamma, AI video, image).  
3. **Response Generation via Custom GPT**:  
   * The collected context is loaded into the updated Custom Lead Generation GPT. GPT, knowing the company's updated capabilities (AI-focused services, relevant cases), generates a highly personalized message.  
4. **Sending and Monitoring**:  
   * Send personalized message and gift with subsequent effectiveness analysis for AI refinement.

**Key Principle for Implementation**:  
Start with manual execution of all stages for understanding the logic, and only then move to automation (e.g., Chrome Extension).

### **Implementation Plan for the New Process**

The plan is divided into stages with clear actions and responsibilities.

#### **Stage 0: Preparation and Administrative Discipline (Immediately)**

* **Suspend Responses**:  
  * Action: Notify the Lead Generation (LG) team of a temporary stop in responding to incoming messages until training is completed. Messages will accumulate.  
  * Responsible: Owners, LG Leaders.  
* **Setting Up Work Environment and Control**:  
  * Action: Ensure all LG staff have VS Code, Dropbox (with personal folders), and WhisperFlow (with a unified shortcut, e.g., Ctrl+Alt).  
  * Daily Check: Verify tool launches, creation of daily .md files in Dropbox, and WhisperFlow activation for recording all discussions. Continue checks until this becomes a habit.  
  * Responsible: Zhenya, Team Leaders.  
* **Forming the Implementation Group**:  
  * Action: Create a cross-functional team for implementation: LG Team Leaders \+ AI Department.  
  * Responsible: Team Leaders, AI Department.
