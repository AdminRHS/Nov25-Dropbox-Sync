## **Slide 15: VPN Usage For Lead Generation**

* **Benefits**  
  * Access location-restricted content  
  * Improve organic search results  
  * Enhance data security & privacy  
  * Anonymize lead generation activities  
* **Security Considerations**  
  * Choose VPNs with AES-256 encryption  
  * Use "No-Log" VPN providers  
  * Avoid free VPNs (security risks)  
  * Enable Kill Switch & DNS leak protection  
* **Recommended VPNs**: ExpressVPN, NordVPN, ProtonVPN

## **Slide 24: Creating LinkedIn Connection Requests**

* **Personalization is Key**  
  * Use recipient's name  
  * Mention common ground or shared interests  
  * Reference their recent activity  
* **Keep It Concise & Clear**

  * Use plain, professional language

## **Slide 31: Follow-Up Strategy \- Email**

* First follow-up: 2-3 days after initial email  
* Second follow-up: 4-5 days later (add value)  
* Third follow-up: 1 week later (alternative CTA)  
* Fourth follow-up: 10-14 days later  
* Final follow-up: 3-4 weeks later  
* More detailed and structured than LinkedIn

---

## **Slide 32: Follow-Up Strategy \- LinkedIn**

* First follow-up: 1-2 days after connection  
* Second follow-up: 3-4 days later (engage with content first)  
* Third follow-up: 1 week later (direct question)  
* Fourth follow-up: 10-14 days later (voice note or informal)  
* Final follow-up: 3-4 weeks later  
* Shorter and more conversational than email

---

## **Slide 35: Scheduling a Call in Google Calendar**

* **Step 1: Initial Setup**  
  * Log into lg@rh-s.com account  
  * Open Google Calendar  
  * Click "Create" \> "Event" \> "More options"  
* **Step 2: Add Event Details**  
  * Title Format: "\[Company Name\] \- Remote Helpers"  
  * Select agreed-upon time (match lead's time zone)  
  * Set 30-minute duration  
  * Add 10-minute and 20-minute notifications

---

## **Slide 36: Calendar Meeting Setup (continued)**

* **Step 3: Add Guests**  
  * sales@rh-s.com (must be included in all client meetings)  
  * Lead's email (verify before adding)  
  * Keep "Modify event" disabled  
  * Ensure "See guest list" is enabled  
* **Step 4: Enter Event Description**  
  * Basic event details  
  * Company website and info (AI-generated)  
  * Lead details (name, LinkedIn, specific requests)  
  * Your name as LG Manager
