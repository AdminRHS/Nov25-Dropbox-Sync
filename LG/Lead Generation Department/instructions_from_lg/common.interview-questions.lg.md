**Interview question** 

1\. What’s the most challenging part for you as a lead generator? 

2\. Regarding the technical aspects of the Lead Generation role, what specific skill gaps do you believe need to be addressed or further developed?

3\. What are your weaknesses as a Lead generator? 

4\. Have you ever had an experience where you were required to work independently and take full ownership of your tasks?

5\. How do you adapt to new processes? / What’s your adaptability process? 

6\. How do you see yourself to adapting to X role/ X industry? How would you approach this? 

7\. What steps would you take to help you adapt to this new position?

8\. Are you comfortable working in a fast paced environment? Do you have experience of working in such environment in the past.

9\. What is the one key thing you are most excited about or looking forward to in joining this position?

10\. Is there something that excites you as a professional?

11\. Do you have any questions from your side?

12\. Can you walk me through a typical workday? How do you find and qualify leads? 

13\. Does your supervisor give you feedback on the quality of the leads you find? Give me an example of the worst feedback you’ve received. 

14\. How would you handle a situation where your team is not satisfied with a lead you’ve identified? 

15\. How do you manage multiple tasks/projects as a lead generator? 

16\. How do you ensure the quality of the leads you generate? Do you follow specific criteria or have a defined process in place?

17\. What would you consider your most significant achievement in your previous role, and why does it stand out to you?

18\. How you’ve managed some competing priorities in your current role?
