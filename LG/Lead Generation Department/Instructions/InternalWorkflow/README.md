This folder contains **internal processes, policies, and operational procedures** for the Lead Generation Department. It serves as the central hub for understanding department fundamentals, daily workflows, performance systems, and specialized processes that keep the team running efficiently.

- Department mission, values, and organizational structure
- Lead generation fundamentals and strategic frameworks
- Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) definitions and targeting criteria
- Core responsibilities and daily workflows
- Meeting setup and call management procedures
- Bonus and incentive system documentation
- Specialized workflows (job sites, rescheduling)
- Service offering materials for client-facing purposes

### Purpose:
These documents define **how the Lead Generation Department operates internally**‚Äîfrom strategic direction to tactical execution. They ensure consistency across the team, clarify expectations, and provide the frameworks that guide daily decision-making.

### üîß **Workflow Highlights**

**Meeting Setup Critical Steps:**
1. Collect lead info before status changes
2. Update CRM (company + lead status to "Event")
3. Log communication entries
4. Create Google Calendar event with specific format
5. Notify lead and sales team
6. Send same-day reminder

**Job Sites Special Rules:**
- Maximum 3 LinkedIn tabs open (avoid account ban)
- Red industries require manual addition or similar industry selection
- Highlight special cases (big companies = red, no leads = blue)
- Always verify country matches LinkedIn (tables may have errors)
- Write "incorrect status" in comments when needed

**Rescheduling Protocol:**
1. Contact lead immediately for new date/time
2. Reschedule in Google Calendar first
3. Update CRM company status (Connected ‚Üí Event with new date)
4. Edit communication entry with new date
5. Inform sales managers in Discord with details
