## OVERVIEW

### Purpose

The Lead Generation Bonus & Incentive System rewards high performance and encourages healthy competition among team members. It's designed to:

- **Motivate Excellence:** Reward quality work and high-volume output
- **Track Performance:** Provide clear, measurable KPIs
- **Build Team Culture:** Foster friendly competition and collaboration
- **Recognize Achievement:** Celebrate milestones and progress

### Core Principles

1. **Transparent:** All bonus triggers and calculations are clearly defined
2. **Fair:** Performance-based, equally accessible to all team members
3. **Timely:** Bonuses paid promptly upon achievement
4. **Motivating:** Structured to encourage continuous improvement

## PERFORMANCE-BASED BONUSES

### Bonus Triggers

#### **Lead Volume Bonus**
- **Trigger:** Add 10 verified leads to CRM
- **Amount:** $10
- **Frequency:** Unlimited (can be earned multiple times)
- **Requirements:**
  - Leads must be verified (LinkedIn profile confirmed)
  - Leads must match ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
  - No duplicates
  - Complete all required CRM fields

**Example:**
- Week 1: Add 10 leads ‚Üí Earn $10

### Dispute Resolution

**If You Disagree with Scoring:**

1. **Check CRM:** Review your activity log for accuracy
2. **Document Issue:** Screenshot/note any discrepancies
3. **Contact Manager:** Report issue via Discord or email
4. **Review Meeting:** Manager investigates within 2 business days
5. **Resolution:** Adjustments made if error confirmed, explanation provided if not

**Common Issues:**
- Lead marked as duplicate (verify uniqueness)
- Call not credited (check CRM status update)
- Points not added (system delay - usually resolves in 24h)
- Leaderboard calculation error (recalculate manually)

**Appeal Process:**
- Manager decision can be appealed to Department Lead
- Department Lead decision is final
- All disputes documented for transparency

## FAQS

### General Questions

**Q: When do I start earning bonuses?**
A: Immediately after completing the 5-day onboarding training. Badge bonuses start from Day 1.

**Q: Are bonuses guaranteed?**
A: Bonuses are performance-based and must meet quality standards. Meeting triggers guarantees payment unless quality issues arise.

**Q: Can I earn multiple bonuses in one day?**
A: Yes! Add 10 leads and book 3 calls in one day = $30 bonus.

**Q: How often is the leaderboard updated?**
A: Real-time updates (within 15 minutes of activity logged in CRM).

---

### Bonus Triggers

**Q: What counts as a "verified lead"?**
A: LinkedIn profile confirmed, company verified, job title accurate, email/phone found or attempted, all CRM fields complete.

**Q: Do follow-up calls count for call booking bonus?**
A: Yes! New call bookings and rescheduled calls both count (as long as they're not duplicates).

**Q: Can I split a batch of leads across weeks?**
A: Yes, but bonus triggers only when you hit 10 verified leads (can be across multiple days/weeks).

**Q: What if a call is cancelled by the client?**
A: You still earn the booking bonus (you did your part). Rebook = earn bonus again.

---

### Leaderboard

**Q: How do I see the leaderboard?**
A: CRM dashboard ‚Üí "Team Performance" ‚Üí "Leaderboard" (updated live).

**Q: Can part-time employees win leaderboard bonuses?**
A: Yes! Leaderboard is based on points, not hours worked. Part-timers can compete.

**Q: What if there's a tie for 3rd place?**
A: Both team members receive $50. We celebrate all high performers.

**Q: Does the leaderboard reset monthly?**
A: Yes, fresh start on the 1st of every month. Previous achievements still tracked for badges.

---

### Payments

**Q: When are bonuses paid?**
A: First week of the following month, with regular salary (e.g., November bonuses paid in early December).

**Q: Are bonuses taxed?**
A: Yes, bonuses are taxed as regular income per local tax laws.

**Q: What if I leave mid-month?**
A: Accrued bonuses up to your last working day are paid in final paycheck.

**Q: Can bonuses be deducted or withheld?**
A: Only for verified quality violations (fake leads, spam, data violations). Legitimate work is always paid.

---

### Quality & Violations

**Q: What happens if I add a duplicate lead?**
A: That lead is removed from your count. If 3+ duplicates in a batch, the entire batch bonus is withheld for review.

**Q: What counts as "gaming the system"?**
A: Adding fake leads, creating duplicate work, inflating metrics, or any intentional manipulation. Results in warning ‚Üí suspension ‚Üí termination.

**Q: How is lead quality measured?**
A: ICP match (70%), data completeness (20%), verification (10%). Must maintain 80%+ average.

**Q: Can I appeal a quality violation?**
A: Yes, follow the dispute resolution process. Legitimate mistakes are understood and resolved fairly.
