## Research Philosophy:

> **Quality over Quantity**: One deeply researched, perfectly matched lead is worth ten poorly researched contacts.

### Core Principles:
1. **Understand before reaching out** - Research thoroughly first
2. **Identify decision-makers accurately** - Wrong person = wasted effort
3. **Validate company fit** - Must match ICP criteria
4. **Personalize based on insights** - Generic outreach fails

## Step-by-Step Process:

### 1. Target Identification
**Objective**: Find companies matching ICP
**Tools**: LinkedIn Sales Navigator, Google, Job Sites, Industry Databases
**Guide**: **find-a-lead.md**

**Key Activities:**
- Define search parameters (industry, size, location)
- Use boolean search operators
- Filter by company signals (hiring, funding, growth)
- Create target company lists

### 2. Company Research
**Objective**: Understand business model, challenges, tech stack
**Time**: 10-15 minutes per company
**Guide**: **instructions.pre-call-research.md**

**Research Checklist:**
- [ ] Company website reviewed
- [ ] Services/products understood
- [ ] Recent news found (funding, expansion, partnerships)
- [ ] Tech stack identified (BuiltWith, Wappalyzer)
- [ ] Company size and locations confirmed
- [ ] Growth indicators noted

### 3. Decision-Maker Identification
**Objective**: Find the right contact person
**Time**: 5-10 minutes
**Tools**: LinkedIn, Company website, Hunter.io

**Target Titles** (varies by offering):
- CTO, VP Engineering, Head of Development
- CEO, Founder (for smaller companies)
- Director of Operations, COO
- HR Director, People Operations (for hiring)

### 4. Deep Dive Research
**Objective**: Gather personalization insights
**Time**: 10-15 minutes before first outreach
**Guide**: **pre-call-research.full.md**

**Lead-Specific Research:**
- [ ] LinkedIn profile reviewed
- [ ] Recent posts/activities noted
- [ ] Background and experience understood
- [ ] Shared connections identified
- [ ] Interests and engagement topics noted
- [ ] Current challenges inferred

### 5. Qualification
**Objective**: Verify lead meets criteria
**Guide**: **lg.process.guidance.md**

**Qualification Criteria:**

âœ… Has identified need/pain point
âœ… Right decision-maker identified
âœ… Budget capacity likely exists
âœ… No recent engagement from our team
âœ… Open to conversations (based on signals)

## Search Techniques:

### LinkedIn Sales Navigator Searches:

**Boolean Operators:**
```
("CTO" OR "Chief Technology Officer" OR "VP Engineering")
AND ("software development" OR "engineering team")
AND NOT ("agency" OR "consultant")
```

**Filters to Use:**
- Company Headcount: 50-500 (adjust based on ICP)
- Geography: Specific countries/regions
- Industry: Select relevant industries
- Company Growth: Hiring signals, funding rounds
- Posted on LinkedIn: Recent activity

### Google Advanced Searches:

**Finding Decision Makers:**
```
site:linkedin.com "CTO" "Company Name"
```

**Finding Tech Stack:**
```
site:stackoverflow.com "Company Name" "hiring"
```

**Finding Recent News:**
```
"Company Name" (funding OR acquisition OR expansion) site:techcrunch.com
```

## Research Quality Standards:

### Minimum Research Requirements:
**Before First Contact:**
- Company website visited
- LinkedIn company page reviewed
- Lead's LinkedIn profile checked
- Basic ICP fit confirmed
- At least one personalization point identified

**Before First Call:**
- Complete **pre-call-research.full.md** checklist
- All qualification criteria verified
- Call objectives defined
- Questions prepared based on research
