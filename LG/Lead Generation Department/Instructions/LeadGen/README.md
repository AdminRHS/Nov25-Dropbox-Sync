# LeadGen Folder

## A) Folder Overview

This folder contains **tactical lead generation playbooks, research methodologies, campaign strategies, and communication templates** for the Lead Generation Department. It provides hands-on guides for finding, qualifying, researching, and engaging prospects through multiple channels including LinkedIn, email, and B2B marketplaces.

### What You'll Find Here:
- Target audience and Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) definitions
- Deep research methodologies and pre-call preparation frameworks
- Campaign strategy guides covering outbound, inbound, and marketplace channels
- Lead qualification workflows and scoring systems
- AI tool strategies and implementation guides
- Email templates and follow-up sequences
- Google Calendar scheduling procedures

### Purpose:
These documents translate strategic objectives into **executable tactics**. They provide the step-by-step processes, templates, and methodologies that lead generators use daily to discover high-potential prospects, conduct deep research, craft personalized outreach, and move leads through the pipeline efficiently.

8. **Email Follow-Up Cadence**: Specific timing guidelines:
   - First follow-up: 2-3 days
   - Second follow-up: 4-5 days later
   - Third follow-up: 1 week later
   - Fourth follow-up: 10-14 days later
   - Final follow-up: 3-4 weeks later
   - Stop after 5-6 unanswered emails, pause 2-3 months

- Account: lg@rh-s.com
- Title: `[Company Name] - Remote Helpers`
- Duration: 30 minutes default
- Reminders: 10 and 20 minutes before
- Guests: sales@rh-s.com + lead email
- Color: Blue (orange for follow-ups)
- Description includes: Company info (ChatGPT-generated), lead details, LinkedIn profile, industry, LG manager name

**Research Documentation Standards:**
- Naming: `CompanyName_DeepResearch_<YYYYMMDD>`
- Storage: Drive ‚Üí Team Lead ‚Üí Deep Research
- Sections: General Info, Deep Research Notes, Summary, Lead Information, LinkedIn Post Insights
- Tools: Perplexity (Research mode), ChatGPT (LG account), Claude (summary generation)

**Email Best Practices:**
- Subject line: Under 50 characters
- Body length: 100-250 words (vs LinkedIn 150 words max)
- Structure: Greeting ‚Üí Introduction ‚Üí Value Offer ‚Üí Details ‚Üí CTA ‚Üí Closing ‚Üí Signature
- Tone: Professional yet approachable (vs LinkedIn conversational/casual)
