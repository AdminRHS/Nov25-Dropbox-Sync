### Initial Login Steps

**Day 1 Setup (15 minutes):**

1. **Receive Credentials**
   - Manager sends email with username and temporary password
   - Check your work Gmail (lg@rh-s.com)
   - May be in Discord direct message

2. **First Login**
   - Navigate to CRM URL
   - Enter username and temporary password
   - System prompts you to create new password

3. **Create Strong Password**
   - Minimum 8 characters
   - Include: uppercase, lowercase, number, symbol
   - Don't use: personal info, common words, "password123"
   - Save in password manager or write down securely

4. **Verify Access**
   - Dashboard should load
   - See your name in top-right corner
   - Main navigation menu visible on left

5. **Bookmark It**
   - Press `Ctrl + D` (Windows) or `Cmd + D` (Mac)
   - Save to bookmarks bar
   - Name it: "ğŸ’¼ CRM"

### Login Troubleshooting

**Issue: "Invalid credentials"**
- âœ… Check caps lock is OFF
- âœ… Copy/paste password from manager's message
- âœ… Try "Forgot password" link
- âœ… Contact manager if still failing

**Issue: "Account locked"**
- Usually after 3 failed login attempts
- Wait 15 minutes and try again
- Or contact manager to unlock immediately

**Issue: CRM loads but shows errors**
- Clear browser cache: `Ctrl + Shift + Del`
- Try different browser (Chrome recommended)
- Disable browser extensions temporarily
- Contact IT support

### 1. Lead & Company Cards

**Company Card Contains:**

- Notes and interaction history

**Lead Card Contains:**

### 3. Time Tracking & Screenshots

**Purpose:**
- Track hours worked for payroll
- Provide transparency for remote work
- Comply with company policies

**How It Works:**
- Clock in at start of day
- CRM tracks time automatically while logged in
- Upload screenshots every 2-4 hours (desktop, not random photos)
- Clock out at end of day

**Screenshot Requirements:**
- Full desktop screenshot (shows you're working)
- Not: Phone photos, cropped images, memes
- Frequency: Every 2-4 hours or as specified by manager

---

### 4. Filters & Search

**Advanced Filtering:**
- Combine multiple filters (Status + Country + Industry)
- Save custom filter views
- Export filtered results to CSV

**Search Tips:**
- Use company name for fastest results
- Search by email to find specific lead
- Use partial names (e.g., "Micro" finds Microsoft)
- Search is case-insensitive
