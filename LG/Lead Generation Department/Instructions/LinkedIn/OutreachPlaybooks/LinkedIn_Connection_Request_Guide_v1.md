### **How to Send a Connection Request to a Lead on LinkedIn: Step-by-Step Guide**

**Step 1: Navigate to the Lead's LinkedIn Profile**

 Locate the profile of the lead you wish to connect with.

**Step 2: Click on "Connect"**  
On the lead\`s profile page, you will see the "**Connect**" button. Click on it to initiate the connection request.

![][image1]

**Additional Information**

If the **'Connect'** button is not visible on the profile page, it may be hidden under the **'More'** option. Simply click on **'More'** to reveal the **'Connect'** button**.**

**![][image2]**

**![][image3]**

**Step 3: Add a Note** 

In the pop-up window, click on the option to **"Add a note"** .

![][image4]

**4\. Compose your Message**  
 In the note field, write your message.

**![][image5]**

**Step 5: Send the Request**

 After composing your message, click the **"Send"** button .

**Step 6: Repeat**  
Repeat this process for each lead.
