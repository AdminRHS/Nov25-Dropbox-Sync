# **Tutorial For SMS Sending Process**

Automotive sending messages through CRM.   
The CRM system already included several templates for the first approach and country codes of needed location.   
Before you start to send messages you have to filter those leads who have phone numbers. But be attentive, there are cases when phone numbers are entered without a country code or with a wrong location \- you have to fix it.   
When you are adding a new lead and fill in the phone number you can send a message at the same time just choosing the needed template.
