# 02_LinkedInOperations

All LinkedIn-specific tactics covering outreach, templates, inbox hygiene, and scalable automation experiments.

## Subfolders
- `OutreachPlaybooks/` - Step-by-step guides for connection requests, follow-ups, SMS handoffs, and channel-specific cadences.
- `Templates/` - Message libraries, tone variations, and GPT-ready snippets.
- `InboxManagement/` - Instructions for keeping the LinkedIn inbox organized and clearing legacy applications.
- `Automation/` - AI tooling notes and automation guardrails for LinkedIn prospecting.

> **Template source of truth:** All production-ready LinkedIn and email templates now live in `01_OnboardingTraining/GeneralGuides/LinkedIn_Message_Templates.md` and `Email_Templates_Guide.md`. The `Templates/` folder is kept only for channel-specific experiments or archived references.
