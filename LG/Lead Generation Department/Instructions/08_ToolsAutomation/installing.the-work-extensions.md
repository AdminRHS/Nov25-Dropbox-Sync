**In our work, we use corporate extensions \- "Google Scrape" and "RE Extension".**

**üîπGoogle Search Extension (Google Scrape) is used to find companies based on specific filters such as country, city, industry, and company size. This tool scrapes the Google search results and automatically compares the results with our CRM database. The extension filters out any duplicates and shows you only the unique companies that are not yet in our CRM, making your lead sourcing process faster and more efficient.**

**üîπLinkedIn Extension (RE Extension) is used for collecting detailed lead information directly from LinkedIn profiles.
