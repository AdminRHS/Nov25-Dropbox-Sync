## **1\. Clocking In and Out**

To ensure accurate time tracking, all employees must **clock in, take breaks properly, and clock out** at the end of the day.

### **How to Clock In (Start Work)**

1. **Log in** to the CRM.  
2. On the **Dashboard**, select **"Remote Helpers"** from the drop-down menu.  
3. Click **"Clock in"** to start your workday.  
4. A **running timer** will indicate that your work has begun.

### **Taking a Break**

1. Click **"Break"** to **pause the timer** when taking a lunch break.  
2. When returning to work, click **"Clock in"** again to resume the timer.

### **Clocking Out (End Work and Submit Report)**

1. Click **"Write report"** at the end of the workday.  
2. Fill in the required fields in the daily work report.  
3. Click **"Send"** to submit your report and **clock out**.
