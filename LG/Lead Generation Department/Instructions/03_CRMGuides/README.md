# 03_CRMGuides

Operational standards for keeping the CRM accurate, timely, and actionable for the wider revenue team.

## Subfolders
- `DataEntry/` - Field-by-field walkthroughs for adding leads, companies, and updates.
- `PipelineManagement/` - Status definitions, movement rules, and follow-up expectations.
- `Reporting/` - Daily report templates and analytics pulled from the CRM.
