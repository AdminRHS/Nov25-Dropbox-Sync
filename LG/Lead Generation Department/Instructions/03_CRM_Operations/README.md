## Field Guidelines:

### Required Fields (Must Complete):
- Company Name (official legal name)
- Company Website (verified URL)
- Industry/Sector
- Company Size
- Location (City, Country)
- Lead Name (First + Last)
- Lead Title (verified position)
- Lead LinkedIn URL
- Lead Email (if available)
- Status (current stage)
- Source (where found)

### Optional But Recommended:
- Company Description
- Technologies Used
- Company Revenue
- Lead Phone Number
- Additional Contacts
- Notes and Observations

## Status Definitions:

| Status | Meaning | When to Use |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| **New** | Just added to CRM | Initial entry |
| **Contacted** | Outreach sent | After sending connection/message |
| **Responded** | Lead replied | Any response received |
| **Qualified** | Meets ICP criteria | After qualification call |
| **Event** | Call scheduled | Calendar invite sent |
| **Client** | Became customer | Never modify! |
| **Not Interested** | Declined offer | Clear rejection received |
| **No Response** | No reply after follow-ups | After 3+ contact attempts |

## Common CRM Tasks:

### Adding a New Lead:
1. Search for duplicates first
2. Use **Add_New_Lead.md** guide
3. Complete all required fields
4. Verify information accuracy
5. Save and set status to "New"

### Updating Lead Status:
1. Open lead card
2. Review current status
3. Use **Changing_the_status_of_a_lead.md**
4. Update status based on latest interaction
5. Add notes explaining change
6. Save updates

### After Successful Call:
1. Follow **CRM_Succesful_Call.md** immediately
2. Update status to "Qualified" or "Event"
3. Document key discussion points
4. Add next steps and action items
5. Update assigned fields

## Troubleshooting:

**Issue: Can't find lead in CRM**
- Try searching by company name
- Search by email or LinkedIn URL
- Check if lead exists under different spelling
- Verify you're in correct CRM view

**Issue: Duplicate detected after creation**
- Contact Team Lead immediately
- Do not delete - mark for review
- Document which entry is more complete
- Let Team Lead merge entries

**Issue: Missing required field**
- Mark lead as "Incomplete" in notes
- Conduct additional research
- Update when information found
- Never guess or make up information
