# 01_OnboardingTraining

Guides and resources for welcoming new team members and giving them the context they need to be productive inside the Lead Generation Department.

## Subfolders
- `GeneralGuides/` - Sequential onboarding flows, first-week agendas, and overview decks.
- `RoleSpecific/` - Playbooks tailored to managers, team leads, or partner roles.
- `ResourceLibrary/` - Reference material, vocab lists, Loom lessons, and quick links.
