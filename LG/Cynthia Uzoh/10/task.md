#### Step 4.3: Send Strategic Follow-Ups
**Instructions:**

   ```

   ```

   ```

   [Link and 1-sentence summary]
   Let me know if you'd like to discuss implications for [their industry].
   ```

#### Step 8.3: Plan A/B Tests

   - Send Version A to first prospect
   - Send Version B to second prospect

   - Track responses for each
   - Don't change other variables

   Results:
   Version A: [Response rate]

   Winner: [A or B]
   Confidence: [%]
   Action: [Adopt B / Keep A / Test again]

## Notes & Tips

### Time Management:
- Use time blocking - stick to the schedule
- Batch similar tasks together
- Take breaks to maintain focus
- Front-load high-value activities (outreach in morning)

### Quality Over Quantity:
- 15 high-quality leads better than 30 low-quality
- Personalization increases response rates significantly
- Better to fully complete follow-ups than rush to add more new leads

### CRM Discipline:
- Log activities in real-time, not end of day
- Complete information now saves time later
- Accurate data = accurate forecasts = better decisions

### Communication:
- Respond to hot leads within 2 hours
- Set expectations on response time
- Keep team informed of blockers
- Ask for help early, not when it's urgent

### Continuous Improvement:
- Track what works and what doesn't
- Test new approaches regularly
- Share learnings with team
- Stay curious and adaptable
