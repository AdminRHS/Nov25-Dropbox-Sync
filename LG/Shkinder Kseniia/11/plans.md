# Daily Work Plan - November 11, 2025

## 1. Morning Review - Newcomers and Juniors Check

### 1.1 Review Newcomer Cards
- **Objective**: Monitor onboarding progress and identify any blockers
- **Actions**:
  - Open the newcomer tracking system/database
  - Review each newcomer's card for:
    - Training completion status
    - Task assignments and progress
    - Integration with team
    - Questions or concerns raised
  - Document completion rates and any issues
  - Identify who needs additional support or follow-up

### 1.2 Review Junior Employee Cards
- **Objective**: Track development and performance
- **Actions**:
  - Check current assignments and deadlines
  - Review quality of work submitted
  - Assess skill development progress
  - Identify training needs or mentoring opportunities
  - Note any performance concerns or achievements

### 1.3 LinkedIn Profile Review
- **Objective**: Verify professional presence and company representation
- **Actions**:
  - Check LinkedIn profiles of newcomers and juniors
  - Verify profiles are complete and professional
  - Confirm company affiliation is correctly listed
  - Review recent activity and connections
  - Ensure compliance with company branding guidelines
  - Document any profile updates needed

### 1.4 Message Review
- **Objective**: Address communications and maintain engagement
- **Actions**:
  - Check internal messaging systems (Slack, Teams, etc.)
  - Review emails from newcomers and juniors
  - Respond to pending questions
  - Follow up on previous conversations
  - Document important discussions or action items
  - Escalate urgent matters if necessary

## 2. Manager Information Update

### 2.1 Compile Information for Managers
- **Objective**: Provide comprehensive updates on team members
- **Actions**:
  - Create summary reports for each newcomer/junior
  - Include the following information:
    - **Performance Metrics**: Task completion rates, quality scores
    - **Progress Updates**: Training milestones, skill development
    - **Attendance & Engagement**: Participation, communication levels
    - **Challenges Identified**: Blockers, areas needing support
    - **Achievements**: Notable successes, quick wins
    - **Next Steps**: Recommended actions, development plans
  - Prepare visual dashboards or reports if applicable
  - Ensure data is accurate and up-to-date
  - Highlight urgent items requiring manager attention

### 2.2 Format and Distribute Reports
- **Actions**:
  - Use standardized templates for consistency
  - Include specific examples and evidence
  - Add recommendations based on observations
  - Schedule or send reports to respective managers
  - Be available for follow-up discussions

## 3. Previous Day Statistics Review (New Strategy)

### 3.1 Analyze Statistics
- **Objective**: Evaluate effectiveness of new strategy implementation
- **Actions**:
  - Access analytics dashboard/reporting system
  - Review key performance indicators (KPIs):
    - **Productivity Metrics**: Tasks completed, hours worked, output quality
    - **Engagement Metrics**: Communication frequency, response times
    - **Learning Metrics**: Training modules completed, assessments passed
    - **Team Collaboration**: Meeting attendance, peer interactions
  - Compare against baseline/previous strategy metrics
  - Identify trends and patterns

### 3.2 Document Findings
- **Actions**:
  - Create statistical summary with visualizations
  - Note improvements and areas of concern
  - Calculate percentage changes from previous period
  - Identify top performers and those needing support
  - Document anomalies or unexpected results

### 3.3 Strategy Assessment
- **Actions**:
  - Evaluate if new strategy is achieving desired outcomes
  - Identify what's working well
  - Note what needs adjustment
  - Prepare recommendations for optimization
  - Schedule review meeting if necessary

## 4. Working Hours Assistance

### 4.1 General Assistance
- **Objective**: Provide continuous support throughout the workday
- **Actions**:
  - Maintain availability via designated communication channels
  - Respond promptly to questions and requests
  - Monitor team chat/communication platforms actively
  - Conduct check-ins with newcomers and juniors
  - Provide guidance on tasks and procedures
  - Facilitate problem-solving and decision-making
  - Connect team members with appropriate resources

### 4.2 Technical Assistance
- **Objective**: Resolve technical issues and enable productivity
- **Actions**:
  - **System Access Issues**:
    - Help with login problems
    - Reset passwords if authorized
    - Grant or request appropriate permissions
    - Troubleshoot authentication errors

  - **Software & Tools Support**:
    - Assist with software installation and configuration
    - Explain how to use company tools and platforms
    - Provide tutorials or documentation
    - Troubleshoot application errors
    - Help with software updates

  - **Hardware Support**:
    - Coordinate IT support for equipment issues
    - Help set up workstations
    - Troubleshoot connectivity problems
    - Arrange replacements if needed

  - **Technical Training**:
    - Provide hands-on guidance for technical tasks
    - Share best practices and shortcuts
    - Create quick reference guides
    - Conduct mini training sessions as needed

  - **Documentation**:
    - Maintain log of technical issues and resolutions
    - Update FAQ or knowledge base
    - Identify recurring problems for systematic solutions

### 4.3 Proactive Support
- **Actions**:
  - Anticipate common issues and address preemptively
  - Check in with team members regularly
  - Monitor for signs of struggle or frustration
  - Offer assistance before being asked
  - Create supportive and inclusive environment
