## üîÑ ITERATION & FEEDBACK

### Design Process:

1. **Generate initial concept** based on prompt
2. **Review against checklist** above
3. **Verify emotional impact** - does it make story clear?
4. **Test memorability** - can you recall it after 5 minutes?
5. **Refine and adjust** based on feedback
6. **Finalize and prepare** for multiple formats
7. **Create variations** for different platforms

### Common Issues to Avoid:

‚ùå Mascot inconsistency between images
‚ùå Overly complex backgrounds
‚ùå Text competing with visual
‚ùå Unclear facial expressions
‚ùå Too cartoonish or unprofessional
‚ùå Missing key story elements
‚ùå Poor lighting or composition
