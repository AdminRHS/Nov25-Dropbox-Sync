## ğŸ¨ COLOR CODING SYSTEM

### Status Colors:
- **Green (#4CAF50)**: Correct way, success
- **Red (#F44336)**: Incorrect way, error
- **Yellow (#FFC107)**: Warning, yellow card
- **Blue (#2196F3)**: Information, neutral

### Setting Colors:
- **Kitchen**: Warm tones (creams, light woods, warm whites)
- **Field**: Natural greens, blues sky, earth tones
- **Store**: Clean, bright, commercial lighting
- **Homes**: Varies by animal (rustic for barn, natural for cave)

### Mascot Color:
- Consistent goat color throughout (suggest: light tan/cream or white)
- Avoid changing mascot color between scenes
