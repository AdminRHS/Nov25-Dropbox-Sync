## Tool Categories

### Primary Tools (Used Daily)

#### ElevenLabs
- **Vendor:** ElevenLabs
- **Purpose:** AI text-to-speech and voice cloning for video voiceovers
- **Category:** AI/Audio Generation
- **Skill Level:** Beginner to Intermediate
- **Cost:** Freemium (Free tier 10k chars/month + paid plans $5-$330/month)
- **Documentation:** https://docs.elevenlabs.io/

#### GLIF
- **Vendor:** GLIF
- **Purpose:** AI workflow automation platform for creative AI tool orchestration and agent-based content generation
- **Category:** AI/Workflow Automation
- **Skill Level:** Beginner to Advanced
- **Cost:** Freemium (Free tier available + paid plans)
- **Documentation:** https://glif.app

#### Leonardo.ai
- **Vendor:** Leonardo.Ai
- **Purpose:** AI image creation with artistic styles and controlnets
- **Category:** AI/Generative AI
- **Skill Level:** Intermediate
- **Cost:** Freemium (Free tier + paid plans $10-$48/month)
- **Documentation:** https://app.leonardo.ai/image-generation

### Secondary Tools (Used Weekly)

#### Midjourney
- **Vendor:** Midjourney Inc.
- **Purpose:** AI image generation for concept art, illustrations, and visual ideation
- **Category:** AI/Generative AI
- **Skill Level:** Intermediate (requires prompt engineering skills)
- **Cost:** Subscription-based ($10/month Basic, $30/month Standard, $60/month Pro, $120/month Mega)
- **Documentation:** https://docs.midjourney.com/

#### Nano Banana
- **Vendor:** Nano Banana
- **Purpose:** AI image generation and manipulation for creative content, thumbnails, and style-specific imagery
- **Category:** AI/Generative AI (Images)
- **Skill Level:** Beginner to Intermediate
- **Cost:** Usage-based (typically accessed via GLIF platform)
- **Documentation:** https://glif.app (accessed via GLIF agents)

#### Discord
- **Vendor:** Discord Inc.
- **Purpose:** Team communication and collaboration platform for text, voice, and video channels
- **Category:** Business Tools / Communication
- **Skill Level:** Beginner
- **Cost:** Free tier available, Nitro subscription $9.99/month, Server Boost from $4.99/month
- **Documentation:** https://discord.com
