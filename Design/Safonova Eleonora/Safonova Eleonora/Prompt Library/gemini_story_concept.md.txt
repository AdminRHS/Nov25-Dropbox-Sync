# RemoteEmployees Instagram Story Concept Export

## Objective
Create a 15-second Instagram Story highlighting **RemoteEmployees**' values of **Global Culture & Flexibility** using a **Minimal, Dynamic, and Professional** visual tone.

---

## 1. Concept Definition

* **Main Theme:** Global Culture & Flexibility
* **Visual Tone & Colors:**
    * **Style:** Minimal, Dynamic, Professional. Crisp text overlays and fast transitions.
    * **Color Palette:**
        * Primary: **Bright Teal/Cyan** (#00C4CC)
        * Secondary: **Clean White** (#FFFFFF)
        * Accent: **Deep Navy Blue/Black** (#1A2C3C)
* **Key Ideas:**
    1.  Work from Anywhere freedom.
    2.  Diverse expertise (Development, Marketing, Design, Translation, etc.).
    3.  Community-focused, supportive global team.

---

## 2. Story Content (15.0 Seconds)

| Time (s) | Script / Captions | Image / Visual Concept | Motion / Animation Suggestions |
| :---: | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **0.0 - 1.5** | **Tired of the cubicle?** | Dreary grey office cubicle. | Subtle "**breaking**" or "shaking" effect on the cubicle. Quick dissolve to white. |
| **1.5 - 3.5** | **Your office is the world.** | Stylized **world map** of glowing Teal dotted lines on a Navy background. 3 Teal location pins blink across the map. | Pins blink; text fades in quickly over the map. |
| **3.5 - 6.5** | **Find your dream remote role.** | Text shrinks as four simple **white line-art icons** pop up on the Navy background. | Icons (Code, Headset, Lightbulb, People) **"pop"** in with a Teal flash. |
| **6.5 - 9.0** | **Development. Design. Translation. Marketing. & More.** | Icons remain. The text cycles rapidly through job categories. | Job titles use a quick **text-scroll/flip** animation, settling on **"& More."** |
| **9.0 - 12.0** | **Global Team. Local Support.** | **Split-screen Montage.** **Left:** Person on a laptop with a sunny outdoor background. **Right:** Video call screen with smiling diverse team members. | Split screen slides in quickly. Text appears in a Teal overlay bar. |
| **12.0 - 15.0** | **Ready to join us?** **[CALL TO ACTION STICKER: Apply Now!]** **rem-s.com** | Final Navy background. White text and the Teal/White CTA sticker. | Final text pulses slightly. The CTA sticker is a prominent, interactive element. |
