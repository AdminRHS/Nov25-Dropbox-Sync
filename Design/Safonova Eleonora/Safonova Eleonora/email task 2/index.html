Who we are

Convenient Brands is a platform of business technology companies that combine industry-specific software with integrated payment processing. Our family of brands supports over 12,000 clients with tools that simplify operations, reduce friction, and drive profitable growth.

What we do

Shop Management Software

Solutions for auto glass, collision, and repair shops to manage jobs, schedules, inventory, and billing.

Payment Processing

Integrated and fee-based payment options, including cards, ACH, and alternative payment models.

Industry Platforms

Technology for auto, government, property management, healthcare, unions, and non-profit organizations.
