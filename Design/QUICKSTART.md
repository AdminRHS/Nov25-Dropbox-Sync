## Your Role

As a member of the Graphic Design team, you'll be responsible for:

1. Create UI/UX mockups and prototypes
2. Design landing pages and web interfaces
3. Develop multi-platform design systems
4. Create graphic assets and visual content
5. Deliver client design projects
