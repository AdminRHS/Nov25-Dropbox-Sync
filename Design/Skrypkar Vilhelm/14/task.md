### Resources and Links:
- [ChatGPT Dialogue with Final Prompt](https://chatgpt.com/share/e/69160177-7828-8002-8c76-1da539186b59)

- Remote Employee Instagram account (for reference examples)

### Instructions:

**Designer Selection Criteria:**
- Available Monday for 2-hour training session
- Good communication and teaching skills
- Experience with ChatGPT image generation
- Patient and supportive teaching approach

**Training Session Structure (2 hours):**

**Part 1: Introduction (20 minutes)**
- Welcome and overview of Remote Employee social media needs
- Explain why lead generators are being trained for this
- Show examples of approved Instagram posts
- Set expectations: they will be able to do this independently

**Part 2: System Walkthrough (30 minutes)**
- Open ChatGPT and demonstrate the full process
- Show how to upload all required materials:
  - Graphics generation prompt
  - Mascot prompt
  - Mascot references
  - Previous post references
  - UI-Kit
  - Design Guidelines
- Explain placeholder system for additional elements
- Show how to specify custom elements if needed

**Part 3: Live Demonstration (20 minutes)**
- Generate a sample post from scratch
- Think aloud through the process
- Show what to check for: mascot consistency, color matching, layout
- Demonstrate requesting changes in follow-up messages

**Part 4: Hands-On Practice (40 minutes)**
- Each lead generator generates their own post
- Designer observes and provides guidance
- Lead generators practice requesting modifications
- Troubleshoot any issues that arise

**Part 5: Q&A and Next Steps (10 minutes)**
- Answer questions
- Explain where to find resources
- Set expectations for designer availability for questions
- Schedule follow-up check-in

**Success Criteria:**
- Each lead generator successfully generates at least 1 on-brand post
- Lead generators understand where to find all resources
- They can articulate the process without prompting
- Generated posts meet 85%+ quality standard
- Lead generators feel confident to try independently

**Post-Training Follow-Up:**
- Designer collects examples of lead generator posts over next 3-5 days
- Note common issues or confusion points
- Document any needed refinements to instruction or prompt
- Report results and recommendations for system improvement

## Task 5: Refine RE Social Media System Based on Lead Generator Testing

### Steps:
1. Attend or review Monday's lead generator training session
2. Observe lead generators' first independent attempts
3. Collect examples of their generated posts
4. Identify patterns in successes and struggles
5. Gather feedback from lead generators and training designer
6. Document specific issues with instruction or prompt
7. Test refinements before implementing
8. Update instruction and prompt documents
9. Communicate changes to lead generators and training designer
10. Monitor results after refinements

### Resources and Links:
- [ChatGPT Dialogue with Prompt](https://chatgpt.com/share/e/69160177-7828-8002-8c76-1da539186b59)
- [RE Mascot References](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1f30GeEfmAl0uX7RYRu_NQejlGy3871WF?usp=drive_link)
- [UI Kit & Design Guidelines](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1hhxZGCJjNSdn2N8Pk9KPA5C4oN1xWZna?usp=drive_link)
- Training session notes and recordings
- Lead generator feedback forms

### Instructions:

**Observation Phase (First Week):**

**Day 1-2: Training Observation**
- Attend training session or review recording
- Note which parts lead generators grasp quickly
- Note where they get confused or need extra help
- Document questions they ask
- Observe their first successful generations

**Day 3-5: Independent Work**
- Lead generators try creating posts on their own
- Collect 3-5 examples from each lead generator
- Note how often they ask for help
- Track how long it takes them to generate a post
- Document any error patterns

**Analysis Phase:**

**Quality Analysis:**
Review collected posts for:
1. **Mascot Consistency:** Does mascot match brand guidelines? Are there variations?
2. **Color Accuracy:** Do colors match UI-Kit? Any deviations?
3. **Layout Quality:** Is layout consistent with approved examples?
4. **Text Handling:** Are placeholders handled correctly? Text readable?
5. **Overall Brand Match:** 85%+ similarity to approved posts?

**Common Issues Identification:**
Look for patterns:
- Do multiple lead generators struggle with the same element?
- Are certain types of posts harder than others?
- Is the placeholder system causing confusion?
- Are reference materials being used properly?
- Is the prompt being followed correctly?

**Feedback Synthesis:**
Gather input from:
- Lead generators: What's hard? What's easy? What would help?
- Training designer: What did they notice? Recommendations?
- Stakeholders: Are posts meeting quality standards?

**Refinement Phase:**

**Instruction Updates:**
Based on findings, consider:
- Adding more specific examples
- Clarifying confusing sections
- Creating step-by-step checklists
- Adding visual guides
- Simplifying complex parts
- Adding FAQ section

**Prompt Updates:**
Consider refining:
- Placeholder explanations
- Color specifications
- Layout instructions
- Mascot integration guidance
- Typography details

**Testing Refinements:**
Before rolling out changes:
1. Test updated instruction/prompt yourself
2. Have one lead generator test it
3. Compare results to previous version
4. Verify improvements address identified issues
5. Make final adjustments

**Implementation:**
- Update documents with clear version numbering
- Notify lead generators and training designer of changes
- Explain what was updated and why
- Provide quick training on changes if significant
- Make old version available for reference

**Post-Refinement Monitoring:**
- Collect examples using updated system
- Compare quality to pre-refinement posts
- Measure improvement in success rate
- Track time efficiency changes
- Gather feedback on improvements

**Success Criteria:**
- 90%+ of lead generator posts meet quality standards
- Time to create post decreases by 20%+
- Lead generators report increased confidence
- Help requests decrease significantly
- System works consistently across different post types
