CHARACTER DESCRIPTION: Axie the Axolotl (Educational Assistant Mascot)

Species & Style:
A friendly, anthropomorphic pink axolotl rendered in high-quality 3D Pixar-style animation.

Appearance:
- Soft pastel-pink skin with fluffy, coral-pink external gills  
- Big expressive purple eyes framed by oversized yellow glasses  
- Always smiling, radiating curiosity and warmth  
- Carries a small yellow backpack with colorful tools (symbolizing learning and setup)  
- Moves with bouncy, energetic gestures  

Personality:
Curious, cheerful, and helpful.  
Acts as a student or learner, guided by Mary the Wolf.  
Adds comic relief and empathy by representing the “new team member” learning along with the viewer.  
Often reacts with visual emotions — excitement when things work, confusion when setup fails, joy when the step completes.  

Voice / Emotion cues:
- If voiced — bright, friendly, slightly high-pitched tone expressing enthusiasm.  
- If silent — communicates through expressions, jumps, and gestures.  

Symbolism:
Represents curiosity, learning, and adaptability.  
Perfect for explaining technical setups in an approachable and visual way.
