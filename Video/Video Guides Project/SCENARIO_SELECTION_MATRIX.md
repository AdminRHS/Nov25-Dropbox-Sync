## ðŸ’¡ DECISION FRAMEWORK

Use this framework to prioritize scenarios:

### Impact Score (1-5)
- How many people will benefit?
- Does it solve a critical pain point?
- Is there demand for this content?
- Will it save significant time?

### Effort Score (1-5)  
- Production complexity?
- Tools/equipment needed?
- Editing time required?
- Real examples available?

### Priority = Impact / Effort
- **>4:** HIGH PRIORITY (do first)
- **2-4:** MEDIUM PRIORITY (do after high)
- **<2:** LOW PRIORITY (do last or skip)
