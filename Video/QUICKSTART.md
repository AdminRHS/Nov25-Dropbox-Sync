## Welcome to Video Production!

Welcome to the Video Production department at Remote Helpers! This guide will help you get started quickly and understand your role.

## Your Role

As a member of the Video Production team, you'll be responsible for:

1. Edit and produce video content
2. Publish videos across multiple platforms
3. Create multi-platform video strategies
4. Optimize video metadata and SEO
5. Archive and manage video assets
